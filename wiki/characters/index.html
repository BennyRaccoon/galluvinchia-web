    Characters of Galluvinchia

    Galluvinchia is shaped as much by individuals as by gods or geography. This is a record of those who left a mark — heroes, rulers, scholars, champions, and the quietly extraordinary.

    Rulers and Leaders

    Aremedia

    Goddess of Impetus · Ruler of An'Ramoda

    Though a goddess, Aremedia is the most frequently seen of all the divine in Galluvinchia. Those who have laid eyes on her describe electricity running through long golden-like hair, a lean and tall figure always dressed in armor, with an olive branch crowning her presence.

    She rules An'Ramoda directly, choosing her consuls by personal judgment and demanding that the city reflect her values of strength, honor, and relentless effort.

    Lewis "The Collector" Pendeltag

    Wise Consul of An'Ramoda

    One of the three consuls chosen by Aremedia herself. Known for his vast collection of magic items and his encyclopedic knowledge of Galluvinchia's history. Also manages the city's tax collection — a combination of wisdom and wealth that makes him one of the most influential people in the land.

    Zack "The Blade" Armada

    Champion of the Colosseum · Brave Consul of An'Ramoda

    A towering black minotaur with sharp horns and a massive blade worn across his back — a blade no one has ever seen him draw. The reigning champion of the Colosseum for more than ten years, and one of the three consuls of An'Ramoda. Whether he wears his political authority with the same ease as his undefeated record is a matter of debate.

    Martin "The Coin" Goldberg

    Mighty Consul of An'Ramoda

    Owner of An'Ramoda's gold mine, and the third of the goddess's chosen consuls. Commerce and coin are his domain — and in a city where the mine fuels everything, his power is considerable.

    King Fernando Oldreekia

    King of the Lady of Marmaros

    Royal by blood, the Oldreekia family has held power in the Lady of Marmaros since before the gods walked the land. Fernando rules alongside the Academy and the Guild, though the balance of that triangle is never entirely stable.

    The Maneless King

    Beloved Ruler of Lorda Gorda

    The Maneless King has always been a kind ruler who looks forward to his people's wellbeing. He holds four of the ten voting points in Lorda Gorda's assembly — a powerful but not unchecked position. The people of Lorda Gorda genuinely love him, which, in a world of ambitious rulers, is rarer than it sounds.

    Scholars and Mages

    Magister Monica Mars

    Head of the Academy of Magic Wonders · Lady of Marmaros

    One of the most skilled and intelligent mages in Galluvinchia. Under her leadership, the Academy of Magic Wonders has grown from an institution into a public brand — its products seen in every city. Whether this makes her a visionary or simply very good at commerce depends on who you ask. Either way, she is one of the most powerful people in Marmaros.

    Merrion Meyer

    Regent of the Academy of Magic Waves and Dreams · Doormi

    Merrion Meyer leads the more humble but generously spirited academy at Doormi. Where Monica Mars builds empires, Merrion builds opportunities — the first year of study is free for brilliant students, regardless of their background.

    Historical Figures

    Salgu, King of the Beasts

    First Age · Founder of An'Ramoda

    In the Age of the Giants, Salgu built the city of An'Ramoda as a sanctuary where humans, centaurs, and minotaurs could live safely within its tall walls. He is also the namesake origin of the minotaur race — the first minotaur was said to be the son of Salgu himself.

    Lumin Oldreekia

    The Great Leokin · Founder of Lorda Gorda

    The greatest hero of the felinfolk, Lumin Oldreekia was the legendary leader who created the first felinfolk city — now called the Whisk of Lumin — at the south coast of Galluvinchia. He later became the first king of Lorda Gorda. Centuries after, the Whisk holds against sandstorms, pirates, and ghouls, its towers still standing in his legacy.

    Raquel Crepusculiento

    The Last Inventor · Lost Academy of Carbohyrr

    The last known inventor of a long-forgotten academy connected to the Lord of Carbohyrr. Her blueprints and notes survive in hidden places, detailing designs and knowledge that the current age has yet to match. Her most notable creation is spoken of in legends, but the truth of it remains buried.

    Archlich Kogarashi

    The Necromancer · Enemy of the Gods

    Once an archmage of unparalleled fury and ambition, Kogarashi rose after the gods' ascension and denounced them as usurpers — ignorant of the true arcane arts. He forged a pact with powerful mages and waged the Arcane War against the new divine order.

    Panos triumphed — but Kogarashi's body was never found. Hidden somewhere in the depths of Ancho Groncho, the labyrinthine land he crafted himself, his fate remains unknown. His necromantic legacy continues to unsettle the living.

    Notable Individuals

    Agustin of Carzagus

    Baker · Lakobordo

    Famous throughout Lakobordo for his extraordinary croissants. Also, for an old silver pendant shaped like a fist that he carries with him everywhere — a pendant he cannot explain, and whose origin even he does not know. He is a kind, unassuming man who seems entirely unlikely to be important.

    Alice Rockwool

    Adventurer · Legend of the Library of Lakobordo

    Traveling the depths below Lakobordo, according to legend, Alice Rockwool and her companions discovered a timeless library sunken in pale tourmaline dust. A cyclopean construct awaited them. Before the battle began, she stepped forward and declared:

    "Yield to the sword of your masters! Look upon it!"

    And the construct kneeled.

    Silvino

    High Paladin of Panos

    One of Panos's most devoted champions, Silvino serves the god of magic and order. His quest involves the recovery of relics lost by his absentminded deity — objects that Panos believes are important but can no longer remember exactly why.
