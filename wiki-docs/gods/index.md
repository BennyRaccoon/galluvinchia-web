# The Gods

In Galluvinchia, the gods are not myths — they are neighbors.

They walk through markets, argue over debts, fall in love, and wage wars that reshape continents. Their divinity does not place them above mortal concerns; it magnifies those concerns to a terrifying scale.

## About the Gods

The gods of Galluvinchia are broadly categorized by their **domain**, their **stance toward mortals**, and their **age**.

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Domain** | The aspect of reality they govern or embody |
| **Mortal stance** | Benevolent, indifferent, or hostile toward mortals |
| **Age** | Old Gods, Current Pantheon, or Forgotten |
